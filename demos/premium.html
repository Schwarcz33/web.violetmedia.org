At Noir & Co., we believe that extraordinary design begins with listening. Every project is a dialogue between space, light, and the unique aspirations of those who inhabit it. Our approach is rooted in restraint and refinement.

How We Work

Our Design Process

Every project follows a carefully orchestrated journey from initial vision to final reveal. No detail is overlooked, no compromise is made.

01

Discovery & Vision

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your lifestyle, aesthetic preferences, and functional needs. This includes site visits, mood board creation, and establishing the design direction that will guide every decision.

Week 1–2
02

Concept Development

Our design team translates your vision into detailed concept presentations featuring 3D renderings, material palettes, furniture selections, and lighting plans. Every element is curated to create a cohesive, breathtaking narrative.

Week 3–5
03

Refinement & Procurement

With your approval, we refine every detail and begin sourcing. We work with exclusive artisans, premium manufacturers, and bespoke fabricators worldwide to bring each element to life with uncompromising quality.

Week 6–10
04

Installation & Styling

Our team manages every aspect of installation with meticulous precision. From furniture placement to art curation and the final styling details, we orchestrate the transformation of your space into a living masterpiece.

Week 11–14
05

The Reveal

The moment we’ve been building toward. We walk you through your completed space, explaining every design decision and detail. Your reaction is our greatest reward—and the beginning of your story in a new home.

The Final Day
