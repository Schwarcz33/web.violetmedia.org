Our Story

since 2018, never looked back

Bella's Bakery started as a small home kitchen project and grew into one of Melbourne's most loved neighbourhood bakeries. Every loaf, pastry, and cake is handcrafted using locally sourced ingredients.

We believe that great baking is simple — quality flour, real butter, fresh eggs, and a whole lot of patience. No shortcuts, no preservatives, just honest food made the old-fashioned way.
